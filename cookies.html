Cookie Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026 | Last Updated: July 13, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device to store information about your preferences, login status, or browsing behavior. Similar technologies such as local storage and session storage may also be used.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies on the XdoList website for the following purposes. Analytics cookies are off by default and are only loaded after you accept the optional analytics consent banner.

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly, such as remembering your language preference, theme preference, and cookie consent status. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics 4 (GA4) is used to understand how visitors interact with our website. We have enabled GA4 in consent mode so it will not load or set cookies until you grant analytics consent.

3. Cookies We Use

Category Provider Purpose
Essential XdoList Store language preference, theme preference, and cookie consent status.
Analytics Google Analytics Measure website traffic and user interactions anonymously.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies, but doing so may affect the functionality of our website.

To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, you can install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Mobile App

The XdoList mobile app does not use browser cookies. For information about analytics and data collection in the app, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy, please contact us at support@xdolist.com.
